About The Varholick Lab

The heart of The Varholick Lab is centered on the problem that severe injuries and surgeries often damage peripheral nerves, disconnecting the brain from the body. Our goal is to REPAIR: REstore nerve Pathways through Adaptive Integration and Regeneration.

Our work aims to improve our understanding of healing and recovery. We are working towards building systems with optimal healing after injury using molecular, cellular, and behavioral therapies. We will then unravel these systems to translate them to humans.
